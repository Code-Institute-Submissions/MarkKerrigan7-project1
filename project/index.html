About The Camp

  • Starting Monday 4th July running through to Friday 8th starting every day at 11:30am and finishing up at 3:30pm
  • The cost of the camp for the week is £50
  • The camp is located at the Omagh Leisure Centre (See map)
  • We have over 15 coaches that are all qualified for teaching children & teens and are all first aid qualified so your kids will be in very safe hands.
  • The lesuire centre has both indoor and outdoor pitches so we are prepared for all weather
  • There will be lunches available for the kids for £2.50 per day

If you have any questions feel free to get in touch using the email / phone number at the bottom or through our social media.
